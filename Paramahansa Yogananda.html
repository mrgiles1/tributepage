A Brief History of Paramahansa Yogananda

1893
In the Beginning

Birth of Mukunda Lal Ghosh (later known as Paramahansa Yogananda) in Gorakhpur, India.

1910
Kriya Yoga Initiation

Initiation into Kriya Yoga by Swami Sri Yukteswar Giri, his guru. More...

1920
Arrival in the US

Paramahansa Yogananda arrives in the United States to attend the International Congress of Religious Liberals in Boston.

1920-1924
US Lecture Tour

Tours the United States, lecturing on the science of yoga and the art of balanced spiritual living.

1925
Self-Realization Fellowship

Establishes the Self-Realization Fellowship in Los Angeles to disseminate his teachings.

1935
Autobiography of a Yogi

Completes his seminal work, "Autobiography of a Yogi," sharing his spiritual journey and experiences with the world. More...

1936
Lake Shrine Construction

Initiates the construction of the Self-Realization Fellowship Lake Shrine in Pacific Palisades, California.

1946
International Religious Congress

Delivers his famous speech at the International Congress of Religious Leaders in Washington, D.C., advocating for the universality of religion.

1950
Last Visit to India

Returns to India for the last time, meeting with dignitaries and spiritual leaders, including Mahatma Gandhi.

1893
Passing in Los Angeles

Paramahansa Yogananda passes away in Los Angeles, leaving a profound spiritual legacy that continues to inspire seekers worldwide. More...
